Our Story

Grilled over real charcoal, every single day.

Take it with you, and enjoy it easy.

Bubus Mtsvadi sits on Akaki Beliashvili, doing one thing well: mtsvadi, grilled the traditional Georgian way over open charcoal until the edges char and the inside stays juicy. Pork or chicken, off the skewer or tucked into fresh bread with herb sauce and red onion.

No shortcuts, no rush — just the real flavor of a Tbilisi grill house, ready to eat in, take with you, or have brought straight to your door.
